Nathan and Hannah met at the age of 17 while attending Rockford High School. In 2012, both Nathan and Hannah decided on Grand Valley State University to pursue their Bachelor's degrees, and here, young love turned into something more.

After four and a half years of joyful dating, filled with numerous trips, lots of ice cream, and countless adventures, Nathan proposed at one of their favorite parks under a blanket of stars, where Hannah, of course, said yes!
