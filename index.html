Parking at Grand Valley

After entering Grand Valley State University’s campus from Lake Michigan Drive, continue straight on Campus Drive. Please park in Lot G. The Cook-Dewitt Center is the small, white building right next to the clock tower.

Directions to The Waldron Public House

Turn right onto Lake Michigan Drive from Grand Valley
Continue east on Lake Michigan Drive for 9.7 miles
Keep right on Fulton Street West, drive for 1.7 miles
Turn right onto Ionia Avenue SW
The Waldron Public House is on the left, a brick building with gargoyles on top

Parking at The Waldron Public House

Metered parking is available on the streets surrounding The Waldron Public House, but can be difficult to find on a Friday evening. These meters range from $1.25 per hour to $1.75 per hour and are only enforced until 6:00 pm, unless they are marked otherwise. If interested in the metered parking, consider downloading the Parkmobile app - it allows you to pay for your meter with a credit card and makes metered parking stress free!

There are three Grand Rapids Parking Services lots located near The Waldron Public House. Two of them are on Ionia at the corners of Oakes and Weston Ave. and the third is on the corner of Weston and Commerce Ave. ranging between $8-$10 per car.
